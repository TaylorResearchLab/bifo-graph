# Source: Kim J, Vaksman Z, Egolf LE, et al. (2023). Germline pathogenic

#   PMCID: PMC9901064

# Specifically: 166 cancer predisposition genes (CPGs) comprising the
# Illumina custom capture (CAP) panel newly designed for the Kim et al.
# 2023 study, as listed in Supplementary eTable 2. The panel was assembled
# pre-analysis to assay rare germline pathogenic variation in the 786
# neuroblastoma cohort.
#
# This is the broader "panel-level" disease-relevant pool. A more conservative
# subset of 54 genes — those with at least one observed P-LP variant in the
# 786-patient cohort, from eTable 3 — is also available as
# kf_nbl_kim2023_observed_reference.txt for sensitivity analysis. See the
# §SM5.2 design note: panel pool tests "all genes selected as candidates";
# observed pool tests "all genes empirically validated in this cohort."
#
# 48 of 166 genes are flagged as DNA repair genes (per eTable 2). DNA repair
# pathway gene set is enriched in NBL cases vs. controls (8.1% vs 5.7%,
# P = 0.011) per the Kim 2023 results.
#
# Format: one HGNC symbol per line. Comments (#) ignored.
# To be intersected with kf_nbl_seed_cuis.txt for §SM5.2 disease-relevant
# subset analysis.

# senior author on the Kim 2023 paper and a co-PI on the U24 grant under
# which this BIFO paper is being conducted. The NBL pool source is
# therefore a peer-reviewed cohort study with internal review continuity.
